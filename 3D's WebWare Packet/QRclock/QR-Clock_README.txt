----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
QR clock is a customized clock experience that utilizes QRs to indirectly display clock like information. An Indirect Clock is a clock based on a form of time but does not display the time directly. Clocks like Modular data-clocks, Bockchain clocks, un-numbered sundails, shadow flute-spanners, color-clocks & faceless clocks are examples of indirect clocks.
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------


You can use the clock face to tell more about the day/night then the actual QRs.

All data used/manipulated:
=============================

Your Position in digital recorded history (your epoch superposition)

Epoch Year & Epoch Time

Sunrise/Sunset (using custom alg)

Sun:Moon Superposition

Month:Year Superposition

the Natural Year Superposition

Your Superposition of the Year

the Epoch:Now superposition

various color chunks formed from different time outputs

the length of the time color chunks

1/4th hour, 1/6th hour & 1/8th hours

Year, Month, Day, Hour, Minute, Seconds. Milliseconds

Reversed time (Hour, Minute, Second, 1/4 hour, 1/6 hour, 2cd grade time)

Not-Today's Date (an possible day-date in our local cluster)

Number of days in the month (pre-determined)

2cd grade readable time

the current 3rd Rite Time Percentage

Various Galactic Hours

Meridian Position (AM/PM)

Base64 Hashing of the current time

Day, Hour & Minute progression percentages

approximate shadow realtive position

the quantum gradient

approximate global average temperature

time-based weather colors (different then the color chunks)
